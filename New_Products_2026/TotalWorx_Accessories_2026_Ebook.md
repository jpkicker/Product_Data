TotalWorx Accessories is the trusted partner for flooring professionals who 
demand performance, reliability, and ease of installation. From adhesives and 
underlayments to moldings and maintenance solutions, our comprehensive 
portfolio is engineered to elevate every installation, residential or commercial.
We don’t just supply accessories, we deliver systems that work harder. With 
innovations like Micro Transitions, Tri Shield Multi-use Underlayment, and 
SpeedLink Spline, TotalWorx Accessories transforms the final touch into a 
lasting impression. Our products are backed by hands-on training, bilingual 
video resources, and strategic partnerships that empower installers and dealers 
alike.
Whether you're on the jobsite or in the showroom, TotalWorx Accessories helps 
you sell smarter, install faster, and grow stronger. Because when every detail 
matters, TotalWorx is how you finish with confidence.
